About Puppy_Coded

Hey! I'm Puppy_Coded :3

I am currently studying a Bachelor of Digital Screen, majoring in Game Arts with a minor in Game Development. I'm still learning to make games, and still finding my flow and inspirations while I complete my study ^^;

One of my main drives is wanting to learn ways to bring my ideas and concepts to life, some of which have been sitting in my head for years. I love making games with cute and cosy styles, that are casual and fun to play.

Something of great importance to me is being able to one day make games with real and accurate LGBTQ+ representation, themes and characters; not only for the sake of awareness but also to help tell the stories and messages that feel important to me.
I also love games with animals or from an animal's perspective, and this is something I would love to integrate into my game designs in the future.

Skill Areas

Games

My main experience is making games in 3D and VR with Unity using C#, though I have some experience with 2D as well. My strongest area is in level design, I love making my environments look great and fun to play in.
I also have some experience with creating and rigging characters in Maya, as well as designing characters from the ground up.

Web

I also have worked as a front end Web Developer for a number of years, learning HTML and CSS, as well as occasional JS where needed. As well as this I learnt a lot about overall website design, and the creation of assets such as logos and animations.
